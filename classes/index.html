Parent and Child

We offer parent and baby and parent and toddler lessons. These sessions are based around fun and interactive games, building water confidence and also allowing the parents to feel confident that they can help their children learn outside of lessons. These are also a very sociable lesson… blah.

Learn to Swim 4+

Our learn to swim program is for children of school age and above, working throught the ASA ‘learn to swim pathway’. Our beginner lessons are a great way for swimmers to build confidence in the water alongside developing basic swimming techique… Up tohigher groups with focus on stroke technique… Classes have a maximum of 5 children per teacher and are allocated based on similar ability.
