About Us

At Chichester swim school our aim is to provide a high standard of swimming tuition in a safe enjoyable and friendly environment where swimmers of any age or ability can reach their full potential.

Our friendly and experienced instructors are committed to providing the best swimming experience for your child. With small class sizes our instructors can get to know each swimmer individually and with gentle encouragement help each swimmer gain confidence and improve their swimming skills and strokes.

We place a great deal of emphasis on giving our children confidence in the water and having fun as well as learning to swim and part of our ethos is constant monitoring of pupil progress and ongoing communication between teachers, parents and pupils. We recognize the importance of parental involvement and are always available to answer any queries. You can discuss a child's progress with the Instructors at any time.

All our instructors are fully qualified and hold the relevent ASA/STA qualifications as well as a recommended life saving qualification for swim teachers and are all CRB checked. We regularly meet to discuss methods and best practices to promote a progressive and dynamic teaching program, as well as attending regular external training sessions, continuously developing our outlook on learning to swim.
